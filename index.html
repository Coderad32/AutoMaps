About Project

Auto Maps is a collaborative workspace and research platform designed for general studies and development. Users can dive into a discovery world, build unique maps, and contribute to an open-source ecosystem. Whether you are a hobbyist or a researcher, Auto Maps gives you the tools to build, train, and share your creations with the community.
